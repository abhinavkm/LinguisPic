LinguisPic


What is LinguisPic?

LinguisPic a web application that is a fun and creative way to help you on your journey to becoming a polyglot master! Learn words that you would like to learn by simplying uploading a picture!


How Does It Work?

Simple! All you have to do is select the language that you are learning, upload a picture and click submit! LinguisPic will then analzye the picture that you submitted, look for key words about that picture and then give you those words in your chosen language.
